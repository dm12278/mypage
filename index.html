Introduction to South American Football

South America have had a big impact in the football world today. Having amazing countries playing in the world cup every 4 years, South America also have their own Mens Football Tournement the Copa America. The Copa America is avilable to the 10 CONMEBOL Teams (Brazil, Argentina, Chile, Urugauy, Ecuador, Bolivia, Peru, Colombia, Venezuela, Paraguay), the tournement is held every 4 years. Some of the previous winners were Argentina in 2021, Brazil in 2019, Chile in 2016, Chile again in 2015, Uruguay 2011, Brazil in 2007 and Brazil again in 2004 but thats just a few.
